**相关模块：** [OH_Camera](https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/capi-oh-camera)

**所在头文件：** [capture_session.h](https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/capi-capture-session-h)
